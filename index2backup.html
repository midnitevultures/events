Family Event

First Friday Art Walk

Aberdeen Art Center welcomes you! The First Friday of every month the Aberdeen Art Center is hosting an art walk with artist's demos, live music and snacks. The Alder Grove Gallery, Hosstyle Ink Tattoo, Friends of the Museum and Leigh's Custom Framing will all be open from 5pm-8pm.

Music

The Trippy Peninsula Tour continues to Aberdeen, Washington. A homecoming show for a couple of ‘Lippy harbor boys’. The Trippy Lips are joined by Portland duo Winnie Carnon. 8:30-11:00 pm.

Music

Brightwire: Live at Mount Olympus Brewing
Combining elements of folk, rock, country, punk and whatever else they happen to get interested in, they weave tales of lives lived in our world today.

Expo

Outdoor Expo
Learn from the hunting, fishing, and camping experts with how-to-seminars, product demonstrations offering tips, techniques and the latest gear. Visitors and lifelong residents alike will learn more about what Coastal Washington and the Olympic Peninsula have to offer.

Expo
